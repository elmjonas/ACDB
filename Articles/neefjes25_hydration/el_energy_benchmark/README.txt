Data for "Humidity effect on the first steps of atmospheric new particles formation: computational study of hydrated molecular clusters

QC_output.tar.gz contains the log/out/xyz files for the single-point electronic energies obtained with the different methods.

data.pkl contains the xyz files, electronic energies, electric dipole moments, and other molecular properties for the various microhydrated
monomers and dimers.

The files of the various folders representing calculations done with the different methods can be found under ACDB/Articles/neefjes25_hydration.
